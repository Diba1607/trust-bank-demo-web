FAQ

Student Account FAQs

What is the minimum age to open a Student Account? +
Students as young as 10 years old can open an account with a guardian. From age 18, the student can independently operate the account without guardian involvement.
Is there really zero minimum balance? +
Yes! We understand students often have limited funds. There is absolutely no minimum balance requirement and no penalty for zero balance, as long as the account is active (at least one transaction per year).
How does the 5% cashback work? +
When you use your TrustBank Student Debit Card to pay at bookstores, stationers, or educational institutions (tuition, coaching centres), 5% of the transaction amount is credited back to your account within 7 working days. Maximum cashback ৳500 per month.
What happens to the account after I turn 25? +
At age 25, you'll receive a notification and your account will automatically upgrade to a TrustBank Savings Account. You'll retain your account number, balance, and transaction history — no need to reapply.
