About TrustBank

For over 27 years, TrustBank has been the financial backbone of millions of Bangladeshi families and businesses — built on trust, powered by innovation.

Who We Are

More Than a Bank — A Partner in Every Chapter of Your Life

Founded in 1998 in the heart of Motijheel, Dhaka, TrustBank Ltd. began with a singular promise: to make financial services accessible to every Bangladeshi, regardless of their background or postal code. Over 27 years, that promise has only grown stronger.

We are one of Bangladesh's top-10 private commercial banks, regulated by Bangladesh Bank and publicly listed on the Dhaka Stock Exchange. With over 200 branches, 500+ ATMs, and a rapidly expanding digital footprint, TrustBank serves 2.4 million customers — from rural households to multinational corporations.

But our story isn't just about numbers. It's about the farmer in Rajshahi who secured his first agricultural loan, the garment worker in Gazipur who sent money home without walking miles to a bank, and the entrepreneur in Sylhet who scaled her business with our SME financing. TrustBank lives in these moments.

Today, we stand at the intersection of tradition and technology — blending decades of banking expertise with cutting-edge digital infrastructure to build the bank Bangladesh deserves for the next 27 years and beyond.

1998

Founded in Dhaka

TrustBank Ltd. receives Bangladesh Bank license and opens its first branch in Motijheel.

On March 17, 1998, TrustBank Ltd. was formally incorporated under the Companies Act 1994 and received its commercial banking license from Bangladesh Bank. The founding board — led by visionary entrepreneur Mr. Azizul Haque — opened the first branch at Dilkusha Commercial Area, Motijheel, Dhaka.

The bank started with an initial paid-up capital of ৳100 crore and a staff of just 87 employees. The first year closed with over 10,000 deposit accounts and ৳45 crore in deposits — exceeding all first-year projections.

2003

Branch Network Expansion

Crosses 50 branches across 8 divisions — one of the fastest-growing private banks in Bangladesh.

Between 1999 and 2003, TrustBank executed an aggressive yet disciplined expansion strategy — opening an average of 10 new branches each year across all 8 administrative divisions of Bangladesh. Priority was given to underserved upazilas and export-oriented industrial zones.

By December 2003, TrustBank operated 52 branches with 500+ employees and 280,000 registered customers. The bank also introduced its first SME loan products, disbursing ৳312 crore to small and medium enterprises nationwide.

2008

First Digital Initiative

Launches internet banking — the first TrustBank online portal enabling account access from home.

In January 2008, TrustBank became one of only a handful of Bangladeshi banks to offer a fully functional internet banking portal. Developed by an in-house team of 22 engineers, the platform enabled customers to check balances, view statements, and transfer funds entirely online.

Within the first six months, over 45,000 customers registered for online banking. The system processed ৳180 crore in online transactions by year-end 2008 — a landmark achievement that proved the appetite for digital banking in Bangladesh years before the smartphone era.

2013

Mobile Banking Launch

TrustBank Mobile app launched — revolutionizing how customers bank on the go.

The TrustBank Mobile app launched in April 2013 on Android and iOS, offering bill payments, fund transfers, loan applications, and a QR-code merchant payment system that predated many competitors by over two years. It was the first step in TrustBank's journey toward becoming a digital-first institution.

The app crossed 200,000 downloads within its first year and was awarded "Best Mobile Banking App in Bangladesh" by BASIS in 2014.

2018

1 Million Customers Milestone

Celebrates serving over 1 million customers. Expands NRB services to 20+ countries.

September 2018 marked a historic milestone: TrustBank's one-millionth account was opened at the Sylhet Amberkhana branch. To celebrate, the bank launched "TrustBank One" — a premium banking tier with dedicated relationship managers and preferential rates.

TrustBank also signed correspondent banking agreements in 20+ countries including the UK, USA, Malaysia, UAE, and Saudi Arabia. Remittance inflows through TrustBank grew by 340% between 2016 and 2018.

2023

AI-Powered Banking

Launches AI-based fraud detection and personalized financial advisory services.

TrustBank deployed a proprietary AI engine in Q1 2023, capable of analyzing 200+ behavioral data points to detect fraud in real time. Within months, fraud losses dropped by 67% — saving the bank hundreds of crore in prevented losses.

TrustBank also introduced "FinBot" — an AI-powered virtual financial advisor accessible through the app, providing personalized savings recommendations and spending insights in both Bangla and English, 24/7.

At the Asian Banking & Finance Retail Banking Awards 2025 in Singapore, TrustBank was named "Best Digital Bank in Bangladesh." Judges cited 98.7% app uptime, an industry-leading NPS score of 72, and instant digital account opening in under 3 minutes as decisive factors.
