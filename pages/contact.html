FAQ

Support FAQ

How do I report an unauthorized transaction? +
Call our fraud hotline immediately at 16575 (24/7). Also block your card from the mobile app. We'll investigate within 24 hours and credit unauthorized charges under our Zero Liability policy.
How long does a complaint resolution take? +
We resolve 90% of complaints within 24 hours. Complex cases may take up to 7 working days. You'll receive updates via SMS and email throughout the resolution process.
Can I visit any branch for my account queries? +
Yes! TrustBank operates on Any-Branch Banking — you can handle most account transactions and queries at any of our 200+ branches, regardless of which branch your account was opened.
