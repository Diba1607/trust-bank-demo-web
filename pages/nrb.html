FAQ

NRB Account FAQs

Who qualifies for an NRB account? +
Any Bangladeshi citizen residing outside Bangladesh, whether on a work visa, permanent residency, or citizenship, qualifies for NRB accounts. You'll need a valid Bangladeshi NID or passport.
Can I open the account from abroad? +
Yes! You can apply fully online. Documents are verified digitally via our secure portal. You can also visit a TrustBank-affiliated correspondent bank in your country.
How quickly are remittances credited? +
International wire transfers are credited within 1-2 business days. TrustBank also has partnerships with major exchange houses for same-day crediting in many countries.
Can my family in Bangladesh access my NRTA account? +
Yes. You can add a nominee who can operate the account on your behalf. You can also set transfer limits and grant specific permissions through our mobile app.
