FAQ

Current Account FAQs

Does a Current Account earn interest? +
Current accounts do not typically earn interest, as they are designed for high-frequency transactions. However, TrustBank offers a special sweep-in facility where excess funds above a threshold are automatically transferred to an FD, earning interest.
What documents are needed for business account opening? +
Required documents vary by entity type. For a proprietorship: Trade License, NID of owner, TIN certificate. For companies: Certificate of Incorporation, Memorandum & Articles of Association, Board Resolution, and KYC of all directors.
How does the overdraft facility work? +
After 6 months of account operation, you can apply for an overdraft (OD) limit. The OD limit is sanctioned based on your account activity and financial statements. Interest is charged only on the amount utilized.
Can I add multiple authorized signatories? +
Yes. You can add multiple signatories with different operational modes (single, joint, or any one of). Each signatory must complete KYC documentation.
