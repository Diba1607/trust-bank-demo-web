FAQ

Fixed Deposit FAQs

Can I break my FD before maturity? +
Yes, premature encashment is allowed. However, interest will be paid at 1% below the contracted rate for the actual period held. No penalty on the principal amount.
What happens at FD maturity? +
At maturity, if auto-renewal is enabled, the FD renews for the same tenure at the prevailing rate. Otherwise, the principal and interest are credited to your linked savings account.
Can I take a loan against my FD? +
Yes. You can avail a loan of up to 90% of your FD value without breaking the deposit. Interest on the loan is 2% above the FD rate. This lets you access funds while still earning FD interest.
Is tax deducted on FD interest? +
Yes, Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) is applicable on FD interest as per the Bangladesh Income Tax Ordinance. If you have a valid tax exemption certificate, please submit it at the branch.
Can I have multiple FDs? +
Absolutely! You can open as many FDs as you wish with different tenures and amounts to ladder your investments for maximum returns and liquidity.
