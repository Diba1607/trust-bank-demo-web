FAQ

Digital Banking FAQ

How do I register for internet banking? +
Visit trustbank.com.bd/netbanking and click Register. You'll need your account number, registered mobile, and NID. Verification takes less than 2 minutes.
Is the mobile app available for both Android and iOS? +
Yes. TrustBank Mobile is available on the Google Play Store (Android 8.0+) and Apple App Store (iOS 14+). It's completely free to download and use.
What is the daily transfer limit? +
The default daily transfer limit is ৳5,00,000 for mobile banking. You can increase or decrease this limit from app settings at any time, up to a maximum of ৳50,00,000 for verified accounts.
What should I do if I forget my password? +
Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen. You'll receive an OTP on your registered mobile number to reset your password instantly.
