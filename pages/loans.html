FAQ

Loans FAQ

What is the minimum salary requirement for a personal loan? +
The minimum monthly income requirement for a personal loan is ৳25,000. For self-employed individuals, we consider average monthly income over the last 12 months.
How fast is loan disbursement? +
Personal loans are disbursed within 24-48 hours of document submission. Home loans take 7-10 working days for property valuation and legal verification.
Can I prepay my loan without penalty? +
Yes, partial prepayment is allowed anytime without penalty. Full prepayment before 12 months may carry a 1% processing fee.
What collateral is required? +
Personal loans up to ৳5 lakhs are unsecured. Higher amounts may require a guarantor or asset as security. Home loans are secured by the property itself.
