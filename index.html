FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open an account with TrustBank? +
You can open an account fully online through our website or mobile app in under 10 minutes. You'll need your NID, a selfie, and proof of address. Alternatively, visit any of our 200+ branches.
Is my money safe with TrustBank? +
Absolutely. TrustBank is licensed and regulated by Bangladesh Bank. All deposits are protected under the Deposit Insurance Scheme up to BDT 1,00,000. We use 256-bit encryption and real-time fraud detection.
What documents do I need to open an account? +
For Bangladeshi residents: National ID card, one recent passport-size photo, and a valid utility bill or rent agreement for address verification. For NRBs, a valid passport and overseas address proof is required.
How long does account opening take? +
Online account opening is typically completed within 24 hours of document verification. For branch openings, the account is usually activated the same day.
Does TrustBank charge for online transfers? +
TrustBank-to-TrustBank transfers are completely free. For BEFTN and RTGS transfers, nominal charges apply as per Bangladesh Bank guidelines. Check our Rates & Fees page for the full schedule.
