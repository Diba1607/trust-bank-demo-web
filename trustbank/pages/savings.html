FAQ

Savings Account FAQs

What is the minimum balance for a Savings Account? +
The minimum opening balance is ৳500 and the minimum maintaining balance is ৳1,000. If your balance falls below ৳1,000, a service charge of ৳250 per half-year applies.
How is interest calculated? +
Interest is calculated on the daily closing balance and credited to your account monthly. At 5.25% p.a., a balance of ৳1,00,000 earns approximately ৳437 per month.
Can I open a joint savings account? +
Yes! Joint accounts are available with "Either or Survivor" and "Joint" operation modes. Both account holders must provide KYC documents.
How many withdrawals can I make per month? +
There is no limit on online/mobile banking transfers. ATM withdrawals are free up to 5 times per month; additional withdrawals incur a nominal charge.
Can a minor open a Savings Account? +
Yes, minors can have a savings account operated by a guardian. Upon reaching 18, the account can be converted to a regular savings account. Check our Student Account for special minor benefits.
