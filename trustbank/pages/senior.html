FAQ

Senior Account FAQs

At what age can I open a Senior Citizen Account? +
The account is available for customers aged 60 years and above. You'll need to provide proof of age (NID or birth certificate) when applying.
Can I convert my existing savings account? +
Yes! If you already have a TrustBank savings account and are 60+, you can convert it to a Senior Citizen Account at any branch or online. Your account number and balance remain unchanged.
What is doorstep banking? +
For seniors who find it difficult to visit a branch, TrustBank offers doorstep banking services. A bank executive will visit your home for cash deposits, withdrawals, KYC updates, and other select services. Available in major cities.
Are there any charges for the Senior Citizen Account? +
No maintenance charges whatsoever. The annual debit card fee is also fully waived for senior citizens.
